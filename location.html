Directions to the Inn:     

Directions


From I-40, take exit 287 onto Harrison Blvd. Turn South (right if coming from the Airport, left if coming from Raleigh).
Travel 3.5 miles to Chatham St., and turn left onto Chatham St.
After 1 block, turn right onto Academy St.
In ~1/4 mile you'll see The Mayton Inn on the right.
